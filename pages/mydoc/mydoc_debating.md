<h1>How did Uthman compile the Quran?</h1>

<p class=MsoNormal><b>Firstly</b>, Uthman instituted a rule that two witnesses
were required for every portion of the Quran presented.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b>Secondly</b>, Zaid bin Thabit was chosen to lead the
committee as he was the closest companion of the Prophet Muhammad and the most
knowledgeable memoriser of the Quran.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b>Thirdly</b>, whenever a manuscript was witnessed by two
men, the Committee checked it against other manuscripts, their memories, and
the memories of the well-known <span class=SpellE>Huffaz</span> of the Quran.
If the readings were consistent, they were officially transcribed into the
Master Uthmanic Codex.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b>Lastly</b>, after the Master Uthmanic Codex was completed
and agreed upon, all other copies of the Quran were collected and burned to
prevent corruption. This meant that anyone who wanted to copy the Quran would
have to do so directly from the Master Uthmanic Codex.</p>

<p class=MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst style='text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1'><![if !supportLists]><span
style='font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol'><span style='mso-list:Ignore'>·<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><![endif]><span dir=LTR></span>Uthman compared his
personal copy of the Qur’an with the one that was with Hafsah.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle style='text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1'><![if !supportLists]><span
style='font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol'><span style='mso-list:Ignore'>·<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><![endif]><span dir=LTR></span>He requested Aisha to send
him the parchments that were dictated by the Prophet and kept in her house.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle style='text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1'><![if !supportLists]><span
style='font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol'><span style='mso-list:Ignore'>·<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><![endif]><span dir=LTR></span>Uthman wanted to further
authenticate the Uthmanic master codex.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle style='text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1'><![if !supportLists]><span
style='font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol'><span style='mso-list:Ignore'>·<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><![endif]><span dir=LTR></span>Once Uthman was satisfied
with all the comparisons and checks, the compilation was deemed complete.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoListParagraphCxSpLast style='text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1'><![if !supportLists]><span

</span></span></span><![endif]><span dir=LTR></span>Following the compilation,
none of the companions raised any complaints regarding any issues such as
additions or omissions.</p>
